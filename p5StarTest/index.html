++ dancing stars ++

create gorgeous 2D graphics with a few clicks :)

for visitors with keyboards:

  • mouse press to drag the stars
  • press 's' to screenshot (.tif)
  • press 'p' to screenshot (.png)
  • press 'LEFT' and 'RIGHT' to get the stars rotate
  • press 'UP' and 'DOWN' to stop the rotation
  • press 'BLANKSPACE' to reset canvas

otherwise, one may click on the operation buttons...
